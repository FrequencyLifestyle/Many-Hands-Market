i.
Our small, big idea

For the people, by the people.

This is a long-held dream made small enough to begin. A gathering place for the families, growers, makers and healers who want a softer, kinder way of feeding and being fed — one that begins in the soil and ends at the kitchen table, with many hands in between.

You'll find people sharing what they grow, what they preserve, what they spin, what they brew. Skills passed across generations and skills found yesterday. Things to taste, things to take home, things to learn.

It's an experiment in a different kind of marketplace: one that runs on quality, neighbourliness, and the quiet conviction that we can build a freer, kinder way of living together — beginning here, beginning now.

— Karina Clappison, founder & field-keeper

A converted dairy barn at the edge of wheat fields and East Yorkshire light. Run by Karina's daughter — a quietly beautiful place to begin.
